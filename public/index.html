Friday, October 5 2012:

A full day of programming flying robots with
node.js in Berlin (the day before JSConf.eu)!

Maximum of 35-50 people and ~20 EUR ticket price, so get notified when the tickets become available:

What?

NodeCopter.js is a full day event where 30-50 developers will get together in teams of 3.

Each team will receive one Parrot AR Drone 2.0 and spend the day programming and playing with it. At the end of the day, each team will get to show off and explain their work.

Why?

Flying robots and node.js!

No seriously, besides having a lot of fun, this event will be great opporunity to explore overlapping fields like Image Recognition, Artifical Intelligence and teach you about things like PID Controllers.

You'll be suprised about the opportunties to apply these techniques into your regular web stuff.
