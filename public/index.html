What?

NodeCopter.js is a full day event where ~35-50 developers will get together in teams of 3.

Each team will receive one Parrot AR Drone 2.0 and spend the day programming and playing with it. At the end of the day, each team will get to present their work to the other attendees.

Why?

Flying robots and node.js!

No seriously, besides having a lot of fun, this event will be great opportunity to explore overlapping fields like Computer Vision, soak up practical knowledge about things like PID Controllers, the Kalman filter and many other fascinating topics.

You'll be surprised about the opportunities to apply these techniques into your regular web stuff.

Venue

The event will be held at Stadtbad Oderberger, a stunning and historic location featuring 13m ceilings and plenty of room for flying.
